## 1.
Find the Fourier Transform of $$x(t)=\sqcap(t-5)$$. 
$$
    \begin{align*}
    X(f)&=\mathcal{F}\left\{x(t)\right\}=\mathcal{F}\left\{\sqcap(t-5)\right\}\\\vdots\\
    &\left<\:\:\:\:\mathcal{F}\left\{x(t-a)\right\}=X(f)e^{-j2\pi{f}a}\:\:\:\:\right>\\\vdots\\
    &=\text{sinc}{(f)}e^{-j2\pi{f}5}\\
    
    \end{align*}
$$
